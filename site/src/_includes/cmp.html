Congestion in San Francisco

This is data on observed auto and transit speeds in San Francisco—by location and by time of day.
  • Auto speeds are observed on CMP roadway segments using floating car runs and other big data sources.
  • Transit speeds are estimated from Automated Passenger Counting (APC) data on SFMTA buses.

How to use this map

Click a roadway segment to see congestion metrics for it. You can pick different years and times of day on the right panel.
Left-click and drag to pan the map Mouse wheel changes zoom Switch to 3D mode! Right-click and drag to change 3D view.
